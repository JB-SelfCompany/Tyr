Tyr is a revolutionary Android application that provides true peer-to-peer email communication using the Yggdrasil mesh network. Unlike traditional email systems that rely on centralized servers, Tyr runs a complete email server directly on your device.

<b>Key Features:</b>

• <b>True P2P Email</b> - No central servers, no cloud infrastructure. Your device IS the email server.

• <b>Yggdrasil Integration</b> - Built on the Yggdrasil overlay mesh network providing secure, end-to-end encrypted connections.

• <b>Standard Protocols</b> - Exposes SMTP/IMAP protocols on localhost, compatible with standard email clients like DeltaChat.

• <b>Privacy First</b> - All data stays on your device. Encrypted configuration backup with AES-256-GCM encryption.

• <b>DeltaChat Ready</b> - Seamless integration with DeltaChat for instant messaging over email.

• <b>Cryptographic Identity</b> - Your email address is derived from your Ed25519 public key, ensuring authenticity.

• <b>Mesh Networking</b> - Connect via public Yggdrasil peers or configure your own custom peers.

• <b>Battery Optimized</b> - Sophisticated power management with timed wake locks and network monitoring.

• <b>Secure by Design</b> - Password protection using Android Keystore with EncryptedSharedPreferences.

• <b>Backup & Restore</b> - Export and import all settings with optional inclusion of your cryptographic keys.

<b>Technical Details:</b>

The app runs a foreground service that manages the native Yggmail library (Go-based), providing SMTP on port 1025 and IMAP on port 1143 (localhost only). Your mail address format is: &lt;64-hex-characters&gt;@yggmail, cryptographically derived from your Ed25519 key pair.
